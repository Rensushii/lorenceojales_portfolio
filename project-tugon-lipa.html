Tugon Lipa · AI‑Powered Smart Community Platform

2nd Place · Hack the Future: Smart Batangas Province Hackathon Challenge

🏆 2nd Place Winner — Hack the Future: Smart Batangas Province Hackathon Challenge

University of Batangas, Batangas City · November 21–22, 2025

The Problem

In Lipa City, citizens struggled with fragmented public service channels. Reporting a broken streetlight, a drainage issue, or a local concern meant navigating confusing phone trees, visiting barangay halls, or relying on social media posts that often went unanswered. Response times were slow, case updates were opaque, and misinformation spread easily. Local government units (LGUs) lacked a centralized system to track, categorize, and resolve citizen reports efficiently.

The Solution

My team built TugonLipa, an integrated smart community platform. The system combines three core components: a centralized emergency and service hotline, a barangay‑focused social feed, and an AI‑driven assistance agent. Citizens can submit reports with photos, receive real‑time case updates, and access official announcements — all from one unified interface.

The Technical Approach

Reports are automatically categorized by AI and routed to the appropriate LGU or private sector office through a structured ticketing workflow. Each department has its own admin portal for faster response and transparent coordination. An AI agent provides instant support, answering citizen queries, checking report statuses, and guiding users through the system. The platform also serves as a social information hub where barangays post advisories and community updates.

The Results

TugonLipa reduces misinformation, speeds up local government response times, and strengthens citizen trust in public services. By merging communication, automation, and community engagement into one platform, it represents a scalable model for smart, connected governance in Batangas Province.
